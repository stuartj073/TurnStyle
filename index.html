What we do

Low cost advertiser

Our business model is as follows: For a small fee, we will effectively and efficiently ensure that your record store is made known to anyone interested in records, irrespective of where they are situated in the world. It's too often that as a record enthusiast, you are left far short of your desired knowledge of a particular area and thus give up looking for the specific store. This platforms sets out to marry those looking for customers with those looking to dig!

News outlet

As well as our mission statement to support and strengthen brick and mortar shops, it would be useless to neglect those of which that are responsible for the continuation of these stores, i.e the Artists! TurnStyle aims to shed light on all events music related from intimate live performances and guest speaking shows to the day and nighttime parties throughout the city in question. TurnStyle is focused on pushing forward all events of all genres of music in the hope of mitigating the chances of missing out on a gig/concert you might have never even heard of before!
